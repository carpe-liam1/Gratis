About

Gratis is a publication that is founded on a

love of passion.

It is an environment with

the goal to lean into passions, goals, curiosities,

and understandings with the goal of channeling

these efforts into a cohesive product.

A physical

magazine will be released each season compiling

extensive projects.

Our goal is to express and

impress upon others a desire to value non-immediate

things and to act upon these sentiments continuously

and with considerable thought.
